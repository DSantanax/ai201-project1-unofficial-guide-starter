Legend
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*: Foundational course
A: Course administered by OMS-Analytics program, still open to OMSCS students
C: Course administered by OMS-Cybersecurity program, still open to OMSCS students

Notes:
- Most current OMSCS courses are offered in Fall and Spring semesters.
- Summer semesters offer a subset of the current course list.
- Seminars are one credit hour, graded pass/fail, and do not count toward foundational or graduation requirements.
- To continue in the program after the first 12 months from matriculation, students must complete 2 foundational courses with a grade of B or better.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total specialization hours: 18
Core course requirement: 9 hours
Elective requirement: 9 hours
Free electives: 12 hours, any courses offered through the OMSCS program

Notes:
- The pasted specialization page lists all courses that may fulfill the Computing Systems specialization regardless of campus.
- Only courses with bold titles on the Georgia Tech page are offered through the online program, but bold formatting was not preserved in the pasted text.
- Any Core Courses in excess of the 9-hour requirement may be used as Computing Systems Electives.
